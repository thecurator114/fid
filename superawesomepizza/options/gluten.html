About Our Business

A couple of years back, me and my frat, The Big Stewarts, needed some serious fuel for our group. Pizza was on our minds, so we hit up our nearest pizza joint.
The pizza was awful! The crust was tough, the sauce had this waxy texture to it, and the whole pie felt lackluster ( granted it was a large $4 pizza, but come on )
It was in that moment of mediocidy my frat and I decided to open up a pizza joint right on campus so no one has to enjoy cheap, cardboard adjacent pizza.
Ever since then, we've grown as a business as a stand-alone enterprise and have continued to deliver only the highest of quality pizza to college students and causals alike.
